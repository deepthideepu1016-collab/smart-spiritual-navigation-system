🗺 Girivalam Route Map

Tiruvannamalai Girivalam is a sacred 14 KM walking route around Arunachala Hill. Devotees walk through the Girivalam path and visit the 8 Ashta Lingams for spiritual blessings.

🗺 Start Girivalam Route Map

💧 Facilities on Girivalam Route

💧 Water Points

Drinking water points are available along the Girivalam route for devotees.

🪑 Rest Points

Resting points help pilgrims relax during the 14 KM walk.

🍛 Anna Prasadam Centers

Anna Prasadam centers provide free food service for devotees during Girivalam.

🙏 Free Rudraksha Points

Some spiritual service centers provide free Rudraksha and devotional guidance.

🕉 8 Ashta Lingams Information

1. Indra Lingam

Represents prosperity, leadership and divine blessings.

2. Agni Lingam

Represents fire, purification, courage and positive energy.

3. Yama Lingam

Represents discipline, health and protection from fear.

4. Niruthi Lingam

Represents protection from negative energy and difficulties.

5. Varuna Lingam

Represents water, purity, peace and emotional balance.

6. Vayu Lingam

Represents air, life energy, strength and good health.

7. Kubera Lingam

Represents wealth, success, growth and prosperity.

8. Esanya Lingam

Represents wisdom, peace, spiritual growth and moksha.
