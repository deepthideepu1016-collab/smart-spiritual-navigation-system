🗺 Girivalam Route Map

Tiruvannamalai Girivalam is a sacred 14 KM walking route around Arunachala Hill. Devotees walk through the Girivalam path and visit the 8 Ashta Lingams for spiritual blessings.

🗺 Start Girivalam Route Map

🕉 8 Ashta Lingams with Nearby Facilities

1. Indra Lingam

Indra Lingam represents prosperity, leadership and divine blessings.

💧 Water Point: Available nearby
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🍛 Anna Prasadam: Usually available near temple-side service areas

2. Agni Lingam

Agni Lingam represents fire, purification, courage and positive energy.

💧 Water Point: Available nearby
🪑 Rest Point: Limited resting space
🙏 Rudraksha / Devotional Items: Available from nearby shops

3. Yama Lingam

Yama Lingam represents discipline, health and protection from fear.

💧 Water Point: Available on route
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🚻 Toilet Facility: Available at selected public points

4. Niruthi Lingam

Niruthi Lingam represents protection from negative energy and difficulties.

💧 Water Point: Available on Girivalam path
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🍛 Anna Prasadam: Available during crowded/festival days

5. Varuna Lingam

Varuna Lingam represents water, purity, peace and emotional balance.

💧 Water Point: Available nearby
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🚑 Emergency Help: Nearby main road access

6. Vayu Lingam

Vayu Lingam represents air, life energy, strength and good health.

💧 Water Point: Available on route
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🙏 Rudraksha / Devotional Items: Available from service stalls

7. Kubera Lingam

Kubera Lingam represents wealth, success, growth and prosperity.

💧 Water Point: Available nearby
🍛 Anna Prasadam: Available near service areas during Girivalam days
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby

8. Esanya Lingam

Esanya Lingam represents wisdom, peace, spiritual growth and moksha.

💧 Water Point: Available nearby
🪑 Rest Point: Available nearby
🛕 Temple return route: Easy access back towards Arunachaleswarar Temple

💧 General Facilities on Girivalam Route

💧 Drinking Water Points

Water points are available at different places along the Girivalam route to help devotees during the walk.

🪑 Rest Points

Resting areas are useful for elderly devotees and pilgrims walking the complete 14 KM route.

🍛 Anna Prasadam Centers

Anna Prasadam service is commonly available during Girivalam, Pournami and festival days.

🙏 Free Rudraksha Points

Some spiritual service stalls and devotional centers may provide Rudraksha and guidance to pilgrims.

Note: Facility availability may change depending on crowd, Pournami days, festival days and local arrangements. Devotees should also check nearby signboards and local help points during Girivalam.
