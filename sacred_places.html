Other Sacred Places of Tiruvannamalai

Sri Ramana Ashramam

Sri Ramanasramam is the spiritual home of Bhagavan Ramana Maharshi. Thousands of devotees visit this peaceful ashram every year for meditation, self-inquiry, and spiritual growth.

Virupaksha Cave

Virupaksha Cave is located on Arunachala Hill and is famous as the place where Ramana Maharshi spent many years in meditation. It offers a serene atmosphere and beautiful hill views.

Skandashramam

Skandashramam is situated higher up on Arunachala Hill. It is a peaceful meditation spot surrounded by nature and provides breathtaking views of Tiruvannamalai town.

Yogi Ramsuratkumar Ashram

Dedicated to the saint Yogi Ramsuratkumar, this ashram is known for spiritual gatherings, prayers, and a calm environment that attracts devotees from around the world.

Sathanur Dam

Located about 30 kilometers from Tiruvannamalai, Sathanur Dam is a popular tourist destination featuring scenic views, gardens, and a crocodile farm.

Pachaiamman Temple

Pachaiamman Temple is a sacred shrine located at the foothills of Arunachala. It is associated with Goddess Parvati's penance and is known for its peaceful surroundings.
