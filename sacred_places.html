Sri Ramana Ashramam

Sri Ramana Ashramam is one of the most peaceful and spiritually important places in Tiruvannamalai. It is dedicated to Bhagavan Sri Ramana Maharshi, a great saint who taught self-enquiry and inner silence.

Devotees visit this ashram for meditation, prayer, peace of mind and spiritual guidance.

Famous For: Meditation, Ramana Maharshi Samadhi, Peaceful Ashram
Distance: Around 2 km from Arunachaleswarar Temple
Best Time: Morning and evening
Open Route Map: View Route

Virupaksha Cave

Virupaksha Cave is a sacred cave located on Arunachala Hill. Sri Ramana Maharshi stayed and meditated here for many years.

People who climb Arunachala Hill often visit this cave to experience peace and meditation.

Famous For: Meditation cave of Ramana Maharshi
Distance: On Arunachala Hill trekking path
Best Time: Early morning
Open Route Map: View Route

Skandashramam

Skandashramam is another sacred place on Arunachala Hill. Sri Ramana Maharshi lived here with his mother for several years.

From Skandashramam, visitors can see a beautiful view of Tiruvannamalai town and Arunachaleswarar Temple.

Famous For: Meditation, hill view, Ramana Maharshi history
Distance: On Arunachala Hill
Best Time: Morning trekking time
Open Route Map: View Route

Yogi Ramsuratkumar Ashram

Yogi Ramsuratkumar Ashram is dedicated to Yogi Ramsuratkumar, who is lovingly called the Godchild of Tiruvannamalai.

The ashram has a peaceful prayer hall and devotional environment.

Famous For: Yogi Ramsuratkumar, prayer hall, spiritual peace
Distance: Near Tiruvannamalai town
Best Time: Morning or evening
Open Route Map: View Route

Sathanur Dam

Sathanur Dam is a beautiful tourist place near Tiruvannamalai. It is surrounded by hills, water and greenery.

The dam area is known for scenic views, gardens and peaceful surroundings.

Famous For: Dam view, nature, gardens, relaxation
Distance: Around 30 km from Tiruvannamalai
Best Time: Daytime
Open Route Map: View Route

Pachaiamman Temple

Pachaiamman Temple is a famous goddess temple near Tiruvannamalai. Devotees visit this temple for blessings, protection and family welfare.

The temple is connected with local traditions and devotion.

Famous For: Goddess worship, blessings, peaceful temple
Distance: Near Tiruvannamalai
Best Time: Morning and evening
